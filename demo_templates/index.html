MITM Attack Demonstration

Live simulation exploring Man-in-the-Middle attacks, credential theft over HTTP, and how TLS provides confidentiality and integrity.
👩
Alice's App
The victim's perspective. Send money or login to the bank application in either secure or vulnerable modes.
💀
Attacker Console
The hacker's view on the local network. Intercept packets in transit, steal login credentials, or tamper with transactions.
🏛
Bank Dashboard
The server's perspective. Monitor incoming login attempts and track successful or tampered money transfers.
🔐
Crypto Engine
The educational perspective. See the underlying logic: AES, DH Key Exchange, RSA, ECDSA, and Certificate Pinning.

📌 Presenter Instructions

For the best experience during your presentation: Open all 4 windows simultaneously (arranged in a 2x2 grid or on separate monitors). When Alice performs an action, you will instantly see the cascading effects across the attacker's console, the bank's feed, and the live cryptographic analysis engine.

Advanced / Standalone Modules
The workspace also includes arp_spoofer.py, dns_spoofer.py, and a standalone bank_server.py available for deeper command-line exploration outside of this browser-based visual demo.
